Origin

The story behind the shop.

As someone who juggles creativity, structure, and self-care, I often struggled to find tools that felt both functional and grounding. Noted by Nani was born out of a desire to blend intentional design with everyday support — no overwhelm, no pressure, just space to grow.

Founder & CEO

Alana Stull

I'm a designer, planner, and firm believer that peace of mind starts with small, intentional steps. As an avid planner and firm believer in intentional living, I created this shop to help others feel more grounded in their day-to-day lives. Through thoughtfully designed templates, I hope to make self-growth approachable, empowering, and rooted in the realities of daily life.
